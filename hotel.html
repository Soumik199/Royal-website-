Some luxary Hotels and Resorts:

Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur

About it

Set in an 18th-century marble palace encompassing an island on Lake Pichola, this luxury hotel, which once featured in a James Bond movie, is less than 1 km by boat from the City Palace in Udaipur.
Price:7000/-

The Leela Palace Udaipur

About it

Indulge yourself in the luxury and comfort tendered at The Leela Palace, Udaipur. There are 80 rooms that overlook the Lake Pichola or the city Palace. Adorned with fine Indian carpets, it offers perfect lodging experience with incredible opportunities for travelers.
Price:5000/-

The Taj Mahal Palace Mumbai

About it

The Taj Mahal Palace is a 21 years old building in the Colaba region of Mumbai. Situated just 20 miles from the international airport, the hotel has been visited by many famous celebrities from India as well as foreign countries
Price:10000/-

Umaid Bhawan Palace Jodhpur

About it

on 26 acres of gardens, this elegant hotel in a grand building was once home to the Jodhpur royal family. It's 7 km from Bhagat Ki Kothi railway station and 10 km from Mandore Garden.
Price:10000/-

The Oberoi Amarvilas Agra

About it

Set on sprawling, landscaped grounds, this palatial luxury hotel is 1 km from the entrance to the Taj Mahal and 15 km from Pandit Deen Dayal Upadhyay Airport.
Price:10000/-

The Leela Palace New Delhi

About it

Just 1 mile from the famous Sarojini Nagar Market and 3 miles from India Gate, The Leela Palace is perched in the posh area of Chanakyapuri in New Delhi. It is famed for proffering luxurious lodging facilities and warm hospitality.
Price:16000/-

The Oberoi Gurgaon

A luxury property rendering much more than one’s imaginations, The Oberoi Gurgaon is a perfect mixture of luxury, comfort and entertainment. There is a Piano bar amidst the lavish surroundings, serving a variety of food and drinks.
Price:17000/-

The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur

TPerched on the banks of Lake Pichola in the romantic city of Udaipur, The Oberoi Udaivilas is a luxury property that exudes the lifestyles of kings and queens of bygone era. he restaurants at The Oberoi Udaivilas satisfy the taste buds of the guests by serving a variety of Indian, Chinese, Rajasthani and continental dishes.
Price:15000/-

The Oberoi Rajvilas Jaipur

The hotel boasts of 54 rooms which are divided into luxury tents, villas and premier rooms. Constructed in Rajasthani fort style, it offers spa that includes Ayurvedic and western treatments for leisure and relaxation.
Price:19000/-

Trident Gurgaon

Spread amid 7 acres of walkways, verdant beauty and a fountain, Trident is positioned in Gurgaon in the vicinity of the entertainment options for the guests.
Price:19000/-

The Paul Bangalore

Luxurious ambience, relaxing spa treatment, world class facilities and friendly hospitality make The Paul among the best hotels in Bangalore. It is situated in a picturesque setting without compromising on uniqueness and quality.
Price:20000/-

Wildflower Hall Shimla

Positioned at a height of 8250ft and in proximity of Shimla mall, Wildflower is a landmark of superior comfort, with spectacular views of Himalayas. It offers tranquil surroundings that make it an idyllic place to stay.
Price:21000/-

The Tamara Coorg

Unparalleled hospitality and picture-perfect location awaits your arrival at The Tamara in the mountains in Coorg. It provides an easy access to the must-see attractions of the city and is the perfect choice for tourists seeking peace and comfort.
Price:17000/-

Amanbagh Resort Rajasthan

The Amanbagh experience starts the moment you arrive through verdant beauty and beautiful doors near Alwar in Rajasthan. It features cultural Rajasthani touches, spa, a big swimming pool and library.
Price:19000/-

Orange Country Resort Kabini

Offering state-of-the art facilities, scrumptious cuisines and modern facilities to pleasure and business tourists alike, Orange Country Resort is situated amidst 200 acres of spice and tea plantations. For travelers looking for a luxurious accommodation in Kabini,
Price:19000/-

The Lalit Resort & spa Bekal

A retreat of elegance, warmth and state-of-the-art facilities, The Lalit Resort & Spa is amongst the most popular properties in Bekal, Kerala. The hotel features award winning restaurants, complete health spa, matchless meeting rooms and well appointed rooms.
Price:18000/-

The Oberoi Cecil Shimla

Ranked among the top 25 luxury hotels in India, The Oberoi Cecil is nestled in proximity to the main destinations of Shimla like Summer Hill, Jakhu Shrine, Mall road and Ice Skating. It tenders lavish accommodation options which are divided into deluxe rooms, premier rooms and luxury suites.
Price:23000/-

Vivanta by Taj Madikeri Karnataka

Explore the romantic intimacy and tranquil beauty at one of the finest resorts of Madikeri- Vivanta by Taj. Whether you are here on a pleasure or business trip, it will make your hotel experience marvelous and unforgettable.
